About Oryvex.

TheCrashersTeam is a company known for its software and website solutions. They have developed Oryvex as a redesigned version of their flagship software and web app called Facile. Oryvex represents an evolution and improvement upon their previous offering, providing users with enhanced search filtering capabilities within specific categories. The company's objective with Oryvex is to deliver a powerful and user-friendly tool that simplifies the search process and enables users to find relevant information more efficiently.

Oryvex serves as a refined and upgraded version of their earlier Facile software and web app. Building upon the foundation of Facile, Oryvex offers an improved and more intuitive search filtering experience. By incorporating user feedback and leveraging their expertise, TheCrashersTeam has redesigned the software and web app to provide enhanced functionality and a more streamlined interface.

With its redesigned features, Oryvex empowers users to navigate through vast amounts of information by applying filters within specific categories. By leveraging the lessons learned from Facile, TheCrashersTeam has optimized Oryvex to prioritize specificity and simplicity, allowing users to quickly narrow down their search results and access the information they need.

In summary, Oryvex represents a redesigned version of TheCrashersTeam's previous software and web app, Facile. With its improved search filtering capabilities, Oryvex aims to deliver a more efficient and user-friendly experience for users in their quest for relevant information within specific categories.

By clicking on this button, users can initiate searches within the designated category, streamlining the process of finding information tailored to their interests.

All at once

The button with the list icon in Oryvex is used to open all websites within a specific category. However, it's important to note that this functionality may not work if the user has blocked pop-ups in their browser settings. If pop-ups are blocked, the action of opening all sites in the category may be prevented. In such cases, users may need to adjust their browser settings to allow pop-ups or consider alternative methods to access the websites within the specific category in Oryvex's search filtering tool.
